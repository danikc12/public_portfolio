The "Kitchen" Analogy

Imagine you want to cook a world-class dinner. You don't just dump dirt-covered carrots and whole cows into a pot.

You have to Gather ingredients, Prep them (peel, chop, wash), and then Plate them. That is exactly what ETL does for business data.

02

Transform

"The Cleaning"

Raw data is full of mistakes. Some dates are written as 01/02 and others as Feb 1st. Transformation fixes the format, removes duplicates, and translates everything into a single "language." It’s the difference between a pile of bricks and a finished wall.

03

Load

"The Storage"

Now that the data is clean and organized, we put it into its final home—a Data Warehouse. It’s now ready for a boss to look at a chart, or for an AI to learn from it. You’ve successfully turned "noise" into "knowledge."
